Richard's Collection

My Poem

Clockwork

the crow knows

exactly where to sit

on the yellow bench


the wave

exactly where to break


the jaw that will not

unclench

is fastened perfectly

to the writer's skull


future generations

come like clock work

under the damp

cement arches


to include themselves

in this well-recorded

afternoon

Inspiration

The Cold

The cold seizes me

and I shiver

The wine

overthrows my tears

THe night puts me to bed

and the sorrows

strengthen my resolve

Your name is burning

under a statue

Even when I was with you

I wanted to be here

The rain unhooks my belt

The wind gives a shape

to your absence

I move in and out

of the One Heart

no longer struggling

to be free
